About Us

Set up in 1979, Secure Parking is now a global leader in the car parking industry with an international presence in 12 countries like Australia, New Zealand, Indonesia, China, Singapore, Malaysia, UK, UAE, Lebanon, Cambodia, USA. Globally, over 2000 car parks and 2.5 million parking spaces are under operation. Secure Parking was set up in India 16 years ago and has offices in Mumbai, Delhi NCR and Bengaluru. In India we manage over 250,000 parking bays at shopping malls, offices, commercial complexes, hospitals, and public car parks (municipal, metro, rail). Our philosophy has been to maintain a careful balance between increasing revenue and providing the highest levels of customer service.
